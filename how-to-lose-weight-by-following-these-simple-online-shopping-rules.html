How to lose weight by following these simple online shopping rules

Posted by Admin on 10th May 2014

In order to further maximize the benefits of online grocery shopping, the Tesco Delivery Labs research team has recently developed a guide to help the average online shopper live healthier and shed the pounds.

Here are their suggestions:

Weekly Meal Planning
Meal Planning is an essential part of a daily routine in cutting down non-essential food intake. Planning your meals ahead only requires a small investment of an hour, but comes with great rewards.

To start, monitor the best-priced groceries for the week on Tesco.com.my, and plan the week’s meals accordingly before putting them into your grocery list. While examining the prices, do opt for healthier choice of ingredients by referring to sites such as caloriecounter, bodybuilding.com, livestrong.com & urbanhealth.com.my.

Utilize online information
Nutritional contents of common products are easily accessible online, which can help customers make informed decisions about their purchases. A good nutritional reference is the US-based Recommended Daily Intake (RDI), which is considered to be sufficient to meet the requirements of 97–98% of healthy individuals.

Stick to your budget
Those who are prone to impulse buying may find that doing their grocery shopping online may be less tempted to do so. As a majority of impulse buys comprises chocolates, sweets, and other confectionery, it would follow that online grocery shopping may encourage further weight loss.

Never shop on an empty stomach
When you are hungry, almost everything seems appetizing and appealing to the eye. Snacking on healthy fruit and nuts prior to carrying out grocery shopping – online and offline – will reduce hunger pangs and encourage you to make more rational choices.

Start making the choice to live healthier by shopping for the right groceries at eshop.tesco.com.my
