The Journey Begins
After placing your order and checking out from eshop.tesco.com.my, a confirmation email will be sent immediately.

Did you know?
There are over 17,000 items available online, from groceries to office supplies, and more!

The Picking Process
Once the order has been received, Tesco’s trained pickers go immediately to work in handpicking your items.

Did you know?
We only pick the best groceries based on freshness, quality of packaging, and other aspects. So it’s as good as you being in the store!

Loading Up The goods are loaded up into Tesco’s Delivery Trucks, which will deliver the goods based on the best possible route.

Did you know?
All our delivery trucks are outfitted with three temperature controlled compartments – Frozen (-16°C), Chilled (2°C), and Ambient (25°C)– to ensure your goods are delivered safe, fresh, and on time.

Did you know?
The best route is calculated by Tesco’s delivery system, which includes factors like distance, time, and weight of goods.

Did you know?
Come rain or shine, condos or houses, we’ll deliver your groceries fresh and on time

At Your Doorstep
Once at your doorstep, feel free to inspect the goods. Not happy? Just return them on the spot!

Our Delivery Man only accepts payment by credit card, not cash, to ensure that all transactions are safe, and reliable every time.
