More Experiments

1. Tesco Delivery Lab Test: Running Delivery Man

Summary: We tested the hypothesis that a delivery man on foot can add mobility, versatility, and reduce cost to current methods. However, lab results indicated a dismal success rate in item delivery.

2. Tesco Delivery Lab Test: Teleportation

Summary: A state-of-the-art teleportation device was successfully deployed to deliver items instantly. Unfortunately, the classified project was shut down due to repeatedly disastrous results. Repeat of "Chicken Incident" is not recommended.

3. Tesco Delivery Lab Test: GPS Guide Rocket Cart

Summary: We built a rocket-equipped shopping cart to test the limits of mobility and speed of our delivery methods. A high risk of injury, however, may be a deterrent to successful implementation.

4. Tesco Delivery Lab Test: Security Guard

Summary: We examined alternative payment methods (e.g., cash) by equipping our security guard with a manual Bernoulli Principled-Airborne system. Results indicate that credit-card payment methods remain the safest.
